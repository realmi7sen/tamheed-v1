# First Examination — MATH 106 — 1442 H, Semester 1

## General Information
- **Institution**: King Saud University, Faculty of Sciences, Department of Mathematics
- **Course**: MATH 106 — Integral Calculus
- **Exam**: First Examination (Midterm 1)
- **Academic Year**: 1441–1442 H, Semester 1 (filename 42-1)
- **Duration**: 2H

---

## Questions

1. Use Simpson's rule with $n = 6$ to approximate the integral $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{2\pi} \sin^4(x)\, dx$.

2. Compute the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{\ln(x) + 1}{1 + (x\ln x)^2}\, dx$.

3. Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{x + \sin^{-1} x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\, dx$.

4. Compute the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{1 + x^6}}\, dx$.

5. Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{4 - e^x}}$.

6. Compute the integral $\displaystyle\int \sin^5 x \cos^2 x\, dx$.

7. Find the limit $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to +\infty} \left(1 + \frac{7}{x}\right)^{5x}$.

8. Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int x^2 \log_3 x\, dx$.

9. Compute $\displaystyle\int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{3 - x^2}}\, dx$.

10. Compute $\dfrac{dy}{dx}$ if $y = (\cos x)^x$.

11. Evaluate $\displaystyle\int \frac{7x + 10}{(x + 1)(x^2 - 4)}\, dx$.
