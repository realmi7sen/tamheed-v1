# Midterm Exam — MATH 106 — 1442 H, Semester 2

## General Information
- **Institution**: King Saud University, Math Department

- **Exam**: Midterm
- **Academic Year**: 1442 H, Semester 2

---

## Questions

### Question 1
(2+3+2)

a) Use Simpson's rule, with $n = 4$, to approximate $\displaystyle\int_{0}^{4} \sqrt{8 + x^3}\, dx$.

b) Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{e^x \ln(e^x + 1)}{e^x + 1}\, dx$.

c) Find $\dfrac{dy}{dx}$ if $y = (1 + x^2)^{2x + 1}$.

### Question 2
(3+2+3)

a) Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{2^x\, dx}{\sqrt{4 - 4^x}}$.

b) Compute the integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{x^4\, dx}{\sqrt{x^{10} - 1}}$.

c) Find the indefinite integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{1 - x^6}}$.

### Question 3
(3+3+3)

a) Compute $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + 3x)^{1/x}$.

b) Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int e^x \sin 4x\, dx$.

c) Find $\displaystyle\int (\cos x)^5 (\sin x)^4\, dx$.

### Question 4
(3+3)

a) Evaluate the integral $\displaystyle\int x^3 \sqrt{x^2 - 4}\, dx$.

b) Compute the indefinite integral $\displaystyle\int \frac{2x^2 - 11x + 9}{x^3 - 6x^2 + 9x}\, dx$.
