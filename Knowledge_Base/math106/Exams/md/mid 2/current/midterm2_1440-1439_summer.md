## Questions

### Question 1

(3+3+3)
Evaluate the following integrals

1. $\int\frac{1}{2x\sqrt{1-9x^{2}}}dx$

2. $\int 4x \cos(2-3x)dx$

3. $\int \sec^{9}x \tan^{5}x dx$

### Question 2

(3+3+3)

1. Evaluate the following integral.
   $\int\frac{x+1}{x(x^{2}+4)}dx$

2. Determine if each of the following integral is convergent or divergent. If it is convergent find its value.
   a) $\int_{-\infty}^{0^{-}}\frac{1}{\sqrt{3-x}}dx$
   b) $\int_{0}^{3}\frac{1}{\sqrt{3-x}}dx$

### Question 3

(3+4)

1. Evaluate the following limit. $\lim_{x\rightarrow0^{+}}x \ln x$

2. Determine the area of the region bounded by $y=2x^{2}+10$ and $y=4x+16.$
