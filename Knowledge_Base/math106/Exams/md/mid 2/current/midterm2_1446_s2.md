(3+3+3)

### Question 2

(2+3+3)

a) Compute $\int \sin 4x \cos 2xdx$

b) Find $\int\frac{dx}{x^{3}\sqrt{x^{2}-4}}$

c) Evaluate $\int\frac{4x^{2}+4x+5}{(x+1)(x^{2}+4)}dx$

### Question 3

(3+2+3)
