## Problem

Find $f'(x)$ if $f(x)$ is the given expression.
$$f(x) = \sinh^{-1}(5x)$$

---

## Rule Used

**Derivative of Inverse Hyperbolic Sine (Chain Rule)**
$$\frac{d}{dx}[\sinh^{-1} u] = \frac{1}{\sqrt{u^2 + 1}} \cdot \frac{du}{dx}$$

---

## Solution

**Step 1 — Identify $u(x)$ and find its derivative**
Let $u = 5x \implies \frac{du}{dx} = 5$.

**Step 2 — Apply the derivative rule**
$$f'(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{(5x)^2 + 1}} \cdot 5 = \frac{5}{\sqrt{25x^2 + 1}}$$

---

## Answer

$$f'(x) = \frac{5}{\sqrt{25x^2 + 1}}$$
