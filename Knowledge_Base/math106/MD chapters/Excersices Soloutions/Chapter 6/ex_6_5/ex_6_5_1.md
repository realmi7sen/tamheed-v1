## Problem

Find $f'(x)$ if $f(x)$ is the given expression.
$$f(x) = 7^x$$

---

## Rule Used

**Derivative of General Exponential Function**
If $a > 0$ and $a \neq 1$:
$$\frac{d}{dx}[a^x] = a^x \ln a$$

---

## Solution

**Step 1 — Identify the base $a$**
Here, $a = 7$.

**Step 2 — Apply the derivative rule**
Apply the formula directly:
$$f'(x) = 7^x \ln 7$$

---

## Answer

$$f'(x) = 7^x \ln 7$$
