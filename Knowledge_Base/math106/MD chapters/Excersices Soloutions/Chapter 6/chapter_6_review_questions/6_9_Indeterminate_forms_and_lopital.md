# Chapter 6 Review — 6.9 Indeterminate forms and L'Hôpital's rule

> Problem bank. Each item keeps its original Chapter 6 Review Exercise number.
> Technique: Evaluating limits that yield indeterminate forms, potentially using logarithmic transformations.

**Find the limit, if it exists:**

109. $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(2 - x)}{1 + e^{2x}}$

110. $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin 2x - \tan 2x}{x^2}$

111. $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{x^2 + 2x + 3}{\ln(x + 1)}$

113. $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{x^e}{e^x}$

114. $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to (\pi/2)^-} \cos x \ln \cos x$

117. $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to \infty} (e^x + 1)^{1/x}$
