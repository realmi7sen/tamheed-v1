## Extended Problems (integration by parts)

**EP 2 (a)** Use integration by parts on $\displaystyle\int f(x)\, dx$ with $u = f(x)$ and $dv = dx$ to find:

(i) $\displaystyle\int \ln x\, dx$

(ii) $\displaystyle\int \tan^{-1} x\, dx$

(iii) $\displaystyle\int \sin^{-1} x\, dx$

(iv) $\displaystyle\int \cos^{-1} x\, dx$

(v) $\displaystyle\int \sqrt{x}\, dx$

**EP 1 (b)** Use integration by parts on $\displaystyle\int f^{-1}(x)\, dx$ with $u = f^{-1}(x)$ and $dv = dx$ to show that
$$\int f^{-1}(x)\, dx = x f^{-1}(x) - F(f^{-1}(x)),$$
where $F$ is any antiderivative of $f$. Verify the formula for the functions in EP 2(a).
